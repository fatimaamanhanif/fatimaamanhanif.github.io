Proper sleep

Points to consider for sleeping well:

  • Sleeping in a calming environment.
  • Sleeping required hours according to your age.

There are many benefits of good sleep like:

  • improves focus,
  • improves mood,
  • reduces stress, etc.

Improves focus

Having high focus is significant in various situations like driving, exams,etc. Constantly zoning out due to lack of sleep can be dangerous when you are driving or cooking.

Improves mood

Have you noticed how when you are sleep deprived you get irritated way easily?

Reduces stress

Did you ever think why your parents ask you to go to bed early before an important exam or presentation? Because whem you do not sleep enough, you stress more. Next time, rather than staying up late to revise more, go to bed early to score more.
